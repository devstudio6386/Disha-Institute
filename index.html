OUR STORY

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 2014, Disha Institute began with a simple mission: to provide quality education to students preparing for competitive examinations. What started in a small room with just 20 students has now grown into one of the most trusted coaching institutes in the region.

Our journey has been marked by countless success stories, innovative teaching methods, and an unwavering commitment to student success. Today, we are proud to have shaped the careers of over 5,000 students, with 1,200+ securing government positions.
